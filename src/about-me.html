Hello!

My Name is Adam and I'm a developer!

What kind of developer?

A versatile one. Currently, I wear lots of different hats and use whichever one is needed for each situation. I do have a strong background in Front-End Development but I dabble in Back-End Development as well to give me the FULL experience. Through college, bootcamps, books, contracts, freelance work, and previous jobs, I've gained a plethora of various experience. I like to think of myself as a jack of all trades.

I started out studying and working on the server side of things considering a career in Network Engineering but soon came to realize that I wanted to do more and push myself further. Because of this realization, I made the transition into Web and Software Development as I continued to study Cyber Security. I started studying Cyber Security at a community college through online classes and further progressed my development skills via online bootcamps, self teaching, and books. Anything I didn't learn in school but deemed necessary for my career, I taught myself. I often find my hunger for knowledge to be endless.

When reading this, I'm sure it's become apparent that I'm a bit of self-starter. A highly motivated one. Cursed by aspirations of perfection. Although perfection is unrealistic, I like to surround myself with others who can push me further and help me get better. I enjoy working with like minded people who are driven and share the same hunger for growth and knowledge while helping each other along the way.

You know what they say, "teamwork makes the dream work!"
